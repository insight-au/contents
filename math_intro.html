Indian Mathematics

All of us are very fond of getting to the finish line in the easiest way possible.Tat too in solving a problem, we would always like to take the shortest route possible. Arithmetic, in the Indian way, abounds with such shortcut methods and mental calculations. There has also been tremendous work done in the fields of algebra, geometry and trigonometry - so much so that most of what we see in our textbooks today, is only an offshoot of the work our ancestors did.
