What are simultaneous equations ?

Your mom's baking cakes for you. She carefully weighs flour and sugar and mixes them in an appropriate ratio. She uses two different cups to measure them out. On one day, she finds that when she mixes 2 cups of flour and 1 cup of sugar, she gets 1? kg of cake. On another day, she mixes 3 cups of flour and 2 cups of sugar and gets 2? kg of cake. Now, she wants to find the measure of each cup. So, how do you think she found out ?

Well, all she had to do was to form a pair of simultaneous equations and solve them ! See if you can solve it . Check your solution in the above link.

More mathematically, simultaneous equations are a set of 'n' equations in 'n' variables. The aim is to solve for the n variables. For example, consider the two equations.

2x + 3y = 8 --- eqn A
4x + 5y = 14 --- eqn B

In the above case, we wish to solve for x & y, which are the two unknowns.
