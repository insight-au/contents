Division is one aspect of arithmetic that is regarded to be very important and equally cumbersome. Take for example 52 ÷ 9. Doing this by the conventional technique involves going through the 9's tables mentally, till 9x5=45. Then subtracting 45 from 52 to obtain 7 as remainder and 5 as quotient.

How about 200165 ÷ 8987 ? Such problems would give nightmares to many.

Is there an easier and elegant way for division ?
