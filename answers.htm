2) Find the fourth power of 31

First calculate 312

D(1) = 1                                 312 =                      1
D(31) = 6                               312 =                    61
D(3)  = 9                                312 =                  961

314 = (312)2

So, find the square of 961

D(1) = 1                                 9612 =                    1
D(61) = 12                             9612 =                  21(carry = 1)
D(961) = 54                           9612 =                521(carry = 5)
D(96) = 108                           9612 =             3521(carry = 11)
D(9) = 81                               9612 =         923521

 

Thus 314 = 923521
