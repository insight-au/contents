Vyakaranam


Vyakaranam means 'grammar'. This section examines the necessity for a grammar, and how effective it is, in getting across what we want to say, in as simple a way as possible. This section also does a comparison of Indian grammar (Tamil & Sanskrit) and English grammar, stressing on the need for precision and clarity in expression. There exist different rules for different grammars, all put forth in a concise fashion.
